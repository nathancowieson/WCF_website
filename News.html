Harvest Sunday


Our recent  Harvest service saw a few firsts for us! The collection

was uplifted to the sound of the Wurzels ‘Combine Harvester’

song, and we were joined by a few new friends, some of whom

will do well to be invited back...their mugshots are shown here.


We had Farmer Strawthatch (who led the way in dodgy comments

about the wives of the elders), an unnamed duck in a Santa hat

and Indian national, Farmer Pranav, who joined us by some sort

of string and packing tape satellite contraption.


A great collection of items for the Food Bank too.


All in all, a bit different - hope you enjoyed it!
